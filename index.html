Gary Smith

A 1991 Graduate of the Joe Kubert School for Cartoon and Graphic Arts, I have done work on various titles for Valiant/Acclaim comics and wrote/illustrated the "How to Draw Manga/Superheroes" line for the Canson Paper Co.

I am currently producing my own comic books: "The Doe-Nild" & "Tales of J'lleen." These books have been available for sale at Modern Myths Comics in Northampton, MA, The Paint and Pixel Festivals in Northampton, MA, the BingCon in Springfield, MA, as well as a similar event at the Norman Rockwell Museum in Stockbridge, MA.

I also enjoy painting landscapes and romantic themes which have been seen annually at the Mystic Art Show in Mystic, CT, the Milford Art Show in CT, the Burr Mansion in Fairfield, CT, as well as at various other venues scattered throughout the Western Massachusetts region.

I currently reside in South Deerfield, MA, where I tend to my lovely garden and ever-wonderful cat, Zipper.
